Bachelor's thesis "Not about chairs, but could've been." I researched stairs, not as an object, but as a narrative of potential.

The 2 main chapters are completely individual stories and non hierarchical. Neither is supposed to be read first or second, so both are introduced at the same time.
It was a simple curiosity about what stories can be constructed from an object deemed mundane, at least by me. Stairs was an intuitive choice, maybe random, but randomness is an asset considering what I set out to do.
